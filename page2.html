CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Ibarra is a young, wealthy mestizo who has recently returned to the Philippines from seven years of study in Europe
  • He is smart, well-respected, and very idealistic
Maria Clara de los Santos
  • She is a woman of high social status and is believed to be Father Damaso's goddaughter as well as the daughter if Capitan Tiago
  • She is truly Father Damaso's biological child, the result of their scandaous relationship with Capitan Tiago's wife.
Padre Damaso
  • Father Damaso is an elderly, ruthlessly corrupt, Spanish priest who spent almost two decades residing among the native Filipinos.
  • He has spent all that time among them, but the years have done nothing to endear him or give him any sympathy for his "flock".
  • He is profoundly prejudiced, spiteful, and vengeful, and he has no problem using his immense influence to destroy people who wronged him.
